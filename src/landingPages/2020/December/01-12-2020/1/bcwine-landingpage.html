British Columbia Wine Institute (BCWI) Winery Member Registration Form

The BC Wine Institute (BCWI) represents the interests of British Columbia wineries dedicated to producing certified 100% British Columbia grape wine. Of the 282 licensed grape wineries operating in the province today, the BCWI represents 94% of BC VQA Wine sales in British Columbia. Choosing membership in BC’s leading wine trade association is good for the industry and good for your business. Together, we’re stronger.

We’d love to welcome you to the BCWI. To take advantage of our marketing, communications and advocacy efforts, simply complete the form below and submit along with your one-time membership registration fee of $105.00. BCWI membership is contingent on approval from the BCWI Board of Directors. Once your registration is approved, we will contact you directly.
