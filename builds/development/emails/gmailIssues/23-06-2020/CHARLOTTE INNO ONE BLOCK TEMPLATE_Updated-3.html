The Big One

A breakdown on the day’s biggest Inno story.

Article Title
Always has a local focus, preferably based on a story we have written. If there’s no big local news, then look for a local voice or other connection to national news. Add value through analysis and context.

The Big One should not be longer than 400 words, except for extraordinarily difficult or important stories. Most days it should be less than 300. Bold names of people and companies on the first reference. Be sure to contextually link to the main story.
